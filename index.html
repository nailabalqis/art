About Me

I'm Naila Balqis

I am a student aged 13 years. I was first known about art since i was 9 years old, although i had started to paint my wall. In the past, i hated drawing class, because i tought that i cant draw anything, but the hateness is ended when i entered 9 years old, for the first time i saw someone draw an anime, in the past i didn't even like anime, because i tought they have a same face, but after i watched video about drawing anime, i'm feel interested, because the anime was so cute, finally step by step i did to make them, at the first time all of my friends laught when they saw my picture, but i didn't surrender, i kept spirit and kept draw, until my picture looked good. When i was 11 years old, i decide to study about digital drawing, i felt difficulty because i didn't have enough specs of phone that time, but after a years my father bought me a new phone which was supported for IbisPaint X, and i always post my picture which i made by using IbisPaint X, and after that someone was interested for buying my picture. Now i only use my phone to make an amazing picture, thats all about me, i hope you like it.
